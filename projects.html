Python Project You Love

This is the project my team made using the skills we developed over the time we used Python. It is a translator, complete with four whole languages, none of which have any impact on whether you get into a good school or not. The translations include "uwu;" a language mainly for thes people on the internet who attept to be extremely cute but end up seeming a little weird, "Pig Latin;" a language I'm sure you're familiar with, as it is quite popular among anyone who speaks English, "blood;" a language alluding to the imfamous Blood gang, typically joked about on the internet, and "FerbLatin;" an alteration of Pig Latin that was included in an episode of Phineas and Ferb by the same name, and used as its main plot-point fir the episode. Included at the start of the program is a language select used mainly as a stylistic choice in...well, selecting the language you want to translate to.

The development of this translator was especially difficult when making the Pig Latin translator. The process was difficult at first because we didn't know how to separate the first letter of the word from the rest of it. This was solved, however, by simply looking up what we needed to know, and within the first few searches, we found the solution. After that, we had difficulty displaying the word. In trying to do that, we needed to separate the first letter form the word and print it afterthe rest, followed by the ending "-ay." This was achieved by starting off the print with the word without the first letter. We then added in the first letter and ended the print with "-ay."
